About Us

Building Intelligent Software Solutions

Financial Calculator India is a privacy-first, offline financial toolkit designed specifically for India. We provide a comprehensive suite of calculators across banking, mutual funds, post office schemes, tax, retirement, insurance, and general financial planning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make financial calculations accessible, accurate, and easy to understand for every Indian. We believe that informed financial decisions start with the right tools, and we are committed to providing those tools free of charge with privacy as a core principle.

What We Offer

Financial Calculator India includes calculators for:

  • Banking: EMI, Loan Comparison, Fixed Deposits (TDR/STDR), Recurring Deposits, Advanced EMI with prepayment analysis
  • Mutual Funds: SIP, SWP, Lumpsum, ELSS, SIP vs Lumpsum comparison
  • Post Office Schemes: PPF, Sukanya Samriddhi, SCSS, KVP, MIS, Post Office RD, Time Deposit, NSC
  • Tax Planning: Income Tax (Old & New Regime), TDS, HRA Exemption, 80C Deductions Tracker, Salary Calculator
  • Retirement Planning: NPS, EPF, VPF, APY, Retirement Planner
  • Insurance: Term Insurance Need, Health Premium Estimator, LIC Maturity Estimator
  • General Tools: Inflation, CAGR, Rule of 72, Goal Planner, Loan Eligibility, Gratuity

Why Choose Financial Calculator India?

  • Privacy First: All calculations are processed locally on your device. No personal data is sent to any server.
  • Offline Capable: Core functionality works without an internet connection.
  • India-Specific: Built for Indian users with support for Indian numbering system (lakh/crore), local bank rates, and tax rules.
  • Live Rates: FD and RD rates synced from major Indian banks (SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Kotak, PNB).
  • Free to Use: Core features are free with an optional ad-free Pro version.

Our Values

  • Accuracy: We strive for accurate calculations using standard financial formulas.
  • Transparency: No hidden fees, no data collection, no surprises.
  • Simplicity: Clean, intuitive interface designed for ease of use.
  • Accessibility: Supporting 13 Indian languages to reach every user.
