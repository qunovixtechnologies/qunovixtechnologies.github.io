1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how English To Hindi Translator, provided by Qunovix Technologies ("English To Hindi Translator," the "App," "we," "our," or "us"), collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect or Process

2.1 Translation Text

When you enter, paste, speak, or scan text for translation, the App processes that text to provide English-Hindi or Hindi-English translation features.

The App may save translation history and favorite translations locally on your device so that you can view, reuse, copy, share, or delete them later.

2.2 OCR Images and Camera Use

If you use camera or gallery translation, the App may access an image you capture or select. The App uses text recognition technology to detect text in the image and then translate the detected text.

Images selected or captured for OCR are processed for text recognition. The App does not create a user account or upload your image to our own servers.

2.3 Voice Input and Speech

If you use voice input or conversation features, the App may use your device microphone and speech recognition services to convert speech into text. The recognized text may then be translated and stored in translation history if the App feature saves it.

Text-to-speech playback may use your device's speech engine to read source or translated text aloud.

2.4 Local Settings and App Data

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Translation history and favorite translations;
  • Dictionary data and word-of-day data;
  • Theme, font size, voice speed, language, and other settings;
  • Temporary cache files used to run the App efficiently.

2.5 Device, Usage, Advertising, and Diagnostic Data

Third-party services used by the App or your device platform may collect device, advertising, diagnostic, crash, performance, or usage information according to their own privacy policies. This may include device type, operating system, app version, approximate network information, advertising identifiers, ad impressions, ad interactions, and fraud-prevention signals.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide translation, dictionary, OCR, voice, conversation, history, favorites, copy, share, and speech features;
  • Download and manage offline ML Kit language models when needed;
  • Save user preferences and app data on the device;
  • Display advertisements where ads are enabled;
  • Open external links such as privacy policy, app rating, and app store pages;
  • Maintain, troubleshoot, secure, and improve the App;
  • Comply with legal obligations and app store requirements.

4. Offline and Online Translation

The App is designed to support offline translation using on-device ML Kit language models and local dictionary data. The first download of English and Hindi language models may require internet access. After the models are available on the device, supported translation features may work offline.

If a Google Translate API key or another cloud translation service is enabled in a future version or custom build, translation text may be sent to that service to provide cloud translation. Such processing would be governed by the relevant service provider's terms and privacy policy.

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google ML Kit

The App uses Google ML Kit for on-device translation and text recognition. ML Kit models may be downloaded to your device when needed. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy.

5.2 Google Mobile Ads / AdMob

The App may display advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. Google may process device, advertising, diagnostic, and usage information to deliver, measure, secure, and improve ads. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy and Google Advertising Policies.

5.3 Device and Platform Services

The App may use device or platform services such as speech recognition, text-to-speech, camera, photo picker, notifications, sharing tools, app store links, browser links, and app store review features. These services are provided by your device, operating system, selected apps, or platform providers and are subject to their own policies.

6. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions depending on your device and the features you use:

  • Internet access: Used for first-time ML Kit model downloads, advertisements, external links, optional online translation, and related online services.
  • Microphone: Used for voice input and conversation speech recognition when you choose to use those features.
  • Camera: Used when you choose to capture an image for OCR translation.
  • Photos, media, or storage: Used when you choose to select an image from your gallery or allow the App to access media needed for OCR translation.
  • Notifications: Used for local app notifications where supported and enabled.

You can manage permissions through your device settings. If you deny or disable a permission, the related feature may not work.

7. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

Translation history, favorites, settings, dictionary data, and cache data are primarily stored locally on your device. They remain there until you delete them in the App where an option is available, clear App data through your device settings, or uninstall the App.

The App includes a cache-clearing feature that can remove temporary cached data. Translation history and favorites are not removed by cache clearing unless you separately delete them or clear App data.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share or allow processing of information only in limited situations:

  • With third-party providers needed for ads, ML Kit model downloads, app store services, platform functionality, diagnostics, or external links;
  • When you choose to share text through your device's native sharing menu;
  • When required by law, legal process, app store rules, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
