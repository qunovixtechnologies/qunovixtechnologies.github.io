The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use its core functionality. You may choose to provide:

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We may collect certain information automatically when you use the App, including:

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data about your device and interactions with ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy. AdMob may use the advertising ID for interest-based advertising. You can opt out of interest-based ads through your device settings.

The App integrates with Google Play Services for in-app reviews and billing. These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 Hive Local Storage

The App uses Hive, a local storage solution, to store your preferences and saved calculation results. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

All calculation inputs and saved results are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain servers or databases that store user information. We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your calculation inputs are not retained by us — they are processed in real-time and discarded after you leave the screen. Saved results remain on your device until you delete them. You can clear all app data at any time through your device's Application Settings.
