Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Financial Calculator India, an app provided by Qunovix Technologies ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to being clear about how information is handled when you use our mobile application (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use its core functionality. You may choose to provide:

  • Calculation inputs: Financial figures you enter for calculation purposes, such as loan amounts, investment values, income, interest rates, and tenure. These are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Saved results: If you choose to save calculation results, they are stored locally on your device using local storage.
  • Settings and preferences: Theme, language, currency display preference, locally synced rate values, and app settings are stored on your device.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We do not require an account and we do not operate our own analytics server. However, third-party services used in the App may automatically collect certain information:

  • Advertising and device data: Google AdMob may collect device information, approximate location, advertising identifiers, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and related data for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and compliance.
  • Google Play service data: Google Play Services may process information when you use Play features such as in-app review prompts.
  • Network request data: When the App syncs FD/RD rate information from an external GitHub-hosted data file, the request may reveal standard technical information such as IP address, device/browser networking metadata, and request time to the hosting provider.

3. How We Use Information

Information is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
  • To display advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To save your preferences and saved calculation results on your device
  • To sync publicly available interest-rate data used by FD/RD calculators
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect or process information under their own policies:

4.1 Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use data about your device and interactions with ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy and Google's advertising technologies information. AdMob may use the advertising ID for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and interest-based advertising where permitted. You can opt out of ads personalization or reset your advertising ID through your device settings.

4.2 Google Play Services

The App integrates with Google Play Services for in-app review prompts. These services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

4.3 GitHub-Hosted Rate Data

The App may fetch publicly available bank FD/RD rate data from a GitHub-hosted file. This is used only to update rate values in the App. We do not send your calculation inputs or saved results with this request.

4.4 Local Storage

The App uses local storage to store your preferences and saved calculation results. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to us.

4.5 Sharing, PDF Export, and External Links

If you use sharing or PDF export features, the App creates the content on your device and hands it to your device's share sheet, print/PDF, or selected external app. The selected external app or service may process that content under its own privacy policy. The App also links to external websites such as RBI, India Post, AMFI, Google, and our website; those websites are governed by their own policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

Calculation inputs are processed locally. Saved results, preferences, and synced rate values are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain our own server or database that stores your calculation inputs or saved results. No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and your device security settings also affect the protection of locally stored data.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your calculation inputs are not retained by us; they are processed in real time on your device. Saved results remain on your device until you delete them in the App or clear the App's data through your device settings. Settings and preferences remain until changed or cleared by you.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Permissions

The App may request or use the following permission:

  • Internet access: Required for loading advertisements, opening external links, and syncing interest rates.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Withdraw consent where applicable
  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below. For data stored only on your device, you can delete saved results in the App or clear the App's data through your device settings.

10. Google Play Data Safety

Google Play may require a Data Safety section for the App. Because third-party SDKs such as Google AdMob may collect or share data for advertising, measurement, fraud prevention, and service operation, the Google Play listing should be kept consistent with this Privacy Policy and the SDKs included in the App.
