Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Privacy-first diary: MyDiary is designed as a personal journal app. Your diary entries are stored locally on your device unless you choose to export them or back them up using a third-party service such as Google Drive.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MyDiary, provided by Qunovix Technologies ("MyDiary," the "App," "we," "our," or "us"), collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the App.

This policy is intended to address common privacy principles, including transparency, data minimization, user choice, security, and rights available under laws such as the GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and similar privacy laws where applicable.

2. Information You Create or Store in the App

The App may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Diary content: Entry titles, body text, dates, moods, tags, templates, favorites, and recycled/deleted-entry status.
  • Attachments: Photos, images, videos, audio recordings, drawings, stickers, or other media you choose to add to diary entries.
  • Profile and account details: Name, email address, local login credentials, and diary lock settings if you choose to use those features.
  • Settings and preferences: Theme, language, date and time format, notifications, default mood, app lock, ad-free status, and other preferences.
  • Export and backup data: Files created when you export your diary or create a backup.

Your diary content is private to your device by default. We do not operate our own server for reading or storing your diary entries.

3. Optional Sign-In, Backup, and Export

If you use Google Drive backup and restore, the App may ask you to sign in with Google and may request access to the Google Drive app data folder. Backups are uploaded to and downloaded from your Google Drive account, and Google processes that information under Google's policies.

If you export entries to files such as TXT, PDF, or backup files, those files may contain diary text and attachments. You are responsible for where you save or share exported files.

4. Information Collected Automatically by Third Parties

The App may use third-party services that can collect device, app, purchase, advertising, diagnostic, or usage information according to their own privacy policies. This may include:

  • Device type, operating system, app version, approximate location derived from network information, and identifiers such as advertising identifiers;
  • Ad impressions, ad interactions, fraud-prevention signals, crash data, diagnostics, and performance information;
  • Purchase and subscription status processed by Google Play or other app store providers;
  • Authentication and file-transfer information when you choose Google sign-in or Google Drive backup.

5. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not intentionally collect your diary entries on our own servers, and we do not sell your diary content. The App does not require you to publish diary entries publicly.

Please avoid adding highly sensitive personal, financial, medical, or legal information to diary entries unless you understand that the information will be stored on your device and may be included in exports or backups you choose to create.

6. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide diary writing, moods, tags, themes, templates, lock, recycle bin, search, calendar, reminders, and attachment features;
  • To save your diary data and settings locally on your device;
  • To create exports and backups when you request them;
  • To show advertisements where ads are enabled and to offer ad-free or Pro features;
  • To process purchases or subscriptions through app store services;
  • To improve App performance, troubleshoot issues, maintain security, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Permissions

The App may request permissions depending on your device and the features you use:

  • Camera: Used when you choose to capture or attach photos.
  • Photos, media, or storage: Used when you choose to attach, save, export, import, or restore diary media or files.
  • Microphone: Used when you choose to record audio for a diary entry.
  • Biometric or fingerprint access: Used for optional diary lock features. Biometric verification is handled by your device; we do not receive your fingerprint or face data.
  • Notifications: Used for optional diary reminders and related notification actions.
  • Internet access: Used for ads, purchases, Google sign-in, Google Drive backup, app store services, and links.
  • Receive boot completed / battery optimization related permissions: Used to keep scheduled reminders working after restart or under device power-management settings where supported.

You can manage permissions through your device settings. Disabling a permission may limit related features.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may use or interact with the following third-party services:

8.1 Google Mobile Ads / AdMob

The App may display advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. Google may process information to deliver, measure, secure, and improve ads. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy and Google Advertising Policies.

8.2 Google Sign-In and Google Drive

If you choose Google Drive backup or restore, the App uses Google Sign-In and Google Drive APIs. Your backup is stored in your Google Drive account, normally in an app-specific storage area. Google's terms and privacy policies apply to those services.

8.3 Google Play Billing and App Stores

Purchases, subscriptions, refunds, app updates, app reviews, and store account information are processed by Google Play or the relevant app store provider under their own terms and privacy policies.

8.4 Device Services

The App may use your device's notification system, biometric prompt, file picker, sharing menu, camera, microphone, media library, PDF viewer, browser, and other operating-system features. These are provided by your device or selected third-party apps.

9. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

Most diary information is stored locally on your device. Locally stored data remains until you delete entries, empty the recycle bin where available, clear App data through device settings, or uninstall the App.

Exported files and backups remain wherever you saved them until you delete them. Google Drive backups can be managed from your Google account or by using the App's restore/backup features where available.

10. Data Sharing

We do not sell your diary entries or personal information. We may share or allow processing of information only in limited situations:

  • With third-party service providers needed for ads, purchases, Google sign-in, Google Drive backup, app store services, diagnostics, or device features;
  • When you choose to export, back up, share, or send diary content outside the App;
  • When required to comply with law, legal process, app store rules, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

11. Security

We use reasonable measures appropriate for the App's local-first design, such as optional diary lock, biometric access through your device, and limiting our own server-side collection. However, no app, device, backup service, network, or storage method is completely secure.

Please protect your device with a strong device lock and be careful when exporting or sharing diary files.

12. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to, or request portability of personal information. Because most App data is stored locally, many privacy choices can be handled by editing or deleting entries, clearing App data, deleting exported files, deleting backups, or uninstalling the App.

You may also contact us using the details below.

13. Children's Privacy

MyDiary is a personal journaling app and is not specifically directed to children under 13. If a child uses the App, a parent or guardian should supervise use, manage permissions, and decide whether diary, media, backup, or sharing features are appropriate.

If you believe a child has provided personal information that should be deleted, please contact us. Because most data is stored locally, you can also delete it from the device.

14. International Transfers

Third-party providers may process information in countries other than where you live. Where applicable, those providers are responsible for using legally recognized safeguards for such transfers.

15. Links to Other Websites

The App may include links to app store pages, privacy pages, support pages, or other websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of external websites.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page and update the "Last updated" date. Please review this policy periodically.
