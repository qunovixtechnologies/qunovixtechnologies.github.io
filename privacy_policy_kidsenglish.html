Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

For parents and guardians: Kids English is intended for children and families. We designed the App to collect as little personal information as practical and to keep learning progress primarily on the user's device.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Kids English, provided by Qunovix Technologies ("Kids English," the "App," "we," "our," or "us"), collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects information when you use the App.

This policy is intended to address common international privacy principles, including transparency, data minimization, security, user choice, children's privacy, and rights available under laws such as the GDPR, UK GDPR, COPPA, CCPA/CPRA, and similar privacy laws where applicable.

2. Who Should Read This Policy

If a child uses the App, a parent, guardian, teacher, or other responsible adult should read this Privacy Policy and supervise the child's use of the App.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The App may store the following information locally on your device using device storage such as SharedPreferences:

  • Child profile name or nickname: If you choose to enter one. We recommend using only a first name or nickname, not a full legal name.
  • Learning progress: Completed lessons, activities, badges, rewards, and similar progress information.
  • Preferences: Settings such as sound preferences or whether a prompt has already been shown.
  • Drawings: Drawings may be saved to the device or gallery only when you choose to save them.

This locally stored information is not sent to our own servers by the App.

3.2 Information You Choose to Share

If you use the device's share feature to share a drawing or app link, the information is handled by your device and the third-party app or service you select. We do not control those third-party services.

3.3 Information Collected Automatically by Third Parties

The App may use third-party services that can collect device, app, advertising, diagnostic, or usage information according to their own privacy policies. This may include:

  • Device type, operating system, app version, approximate location derived from network information, and identifiers such as advertising identifiers;
  • Ad impressions, ad interactions, crash or diagnostic data, and fraud-prevention signals;
  • Information needed to provide app store services, reviews, links, fonts, or platform functionality.

4. Information We Do Not Intentionally Collect

We do not intentionally require children to provide:

  • Full name, address, phone number, email address, school name, or government ID;
  • Photos, videos, microphone recordings, contacts, calendar data, or precise GPS location;
  • Account login credentials or social media profile information.

The App uses text-to-speech functionality for pronunciation and learning support; it does not need microphone recording for this feature.

5. How We Use Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide learning activities, games, drawing tools, pronunciation support, badges, and progress tracking;
  • To save preferences and progress on the device;
  • To show child-appropriate, non-personalized advertisements where ads are enabled;
  • To improve App performance, fix issues, and maintain security;
  • To respond to support requests and comply with legal obligations.

6. Legal Bases Where Applicable

Where privacy laws require a legal basis, we rely on one or more of the following, depending on the context:

  • Consent: For optional permissions, optional profile details, and certain advertising choices where required;
  • Legitimate interests: To operate, secure, maintain, and improve the App in a privacy-conscious manner;
  • Contract: To provide the App features requested by the user or supervising adult;
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable law, app store requirements, child safety rules, or regulator requests.

7. Children's Privacy

Kids English is designed for children and families. We do not knowingly collect direct contact information from children, and the App does not require children to create an account.

Where advertising is enabled, we configure Google Mobile Ads requests for child-directed treatment, users under the age of consent, non-personalized ads, and a general audience ad content rating where supported. These settings are intended to support compliance with children's privacy and advertising rules such as COPPA, GDPR-K, and similar regional requirements.

If you believe a child has provided personal information that should be deleted, please contact us using the details below. Because most App data is stored locally on the device, you can also delete it by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

8. Third-Party Services

The App may use or interact with the following third-party services:

8.1 Google Mobile Ads / AdMob

The App may display advertisements through Google Mobile Ads. We request non-personalized, child-directed ads where supported. Google may still process limited information to deliver, measure, secure, and improve ads. Learn more at Google's Privacy Policy and Google Advertising Policies.

8.2 Google Play Services and App Stores

The App may use Google Play Services or app store features for app reviews, app listing links, updates, and platform functionality. These services are governed by the relevant platform's privacy policy.

8.3 Device Text-to-Speech, Sharing, Gallery, and Browser Features

The App may use your device's text-to-speech engine, native sharing menu, gallery/photo storage, or browser/link-opening tools. These features are provided by your operating system or selected third-party apps and are subject to their own policies.

9. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions depending on platform and feature use:

  • Internet access: Used for advertisements, app links, and related online services.
  • Photos, gallery, or storage: Used only when you choose to save drawings to your device gallery or storage.

You can manage permissions through your device settings. Disabling a permission may limit related features.

10. Data Storage, Retention, and Deletion

Most learning data, such as child nickname, progress, badges, preferences, and drawings, is stored locally on your device. We do not operate a user account database for this App.

Locally stored information remains on the device until you delete it, reset progress where the App provides that option, clear the App's data through device settings, or uninstall the App. Drawings saved to your gallery remain there until you delete them from the device.

11. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share or allow processing of information only in the following limited situations:

  • With third-party service providers needed to operate ads, platform services, app links, reviews, diagnostics, or device features;
  • When you choose to share content through your device's native sharing tools;
  • When required to comply with law, legal process, child safety obligations, or app store rules;
  • To protect the rights, safety, and security of users, children, the App, or the public.

12. International Transfers

Third-party providers may process information in countries other than where you live. Where applicable, such transfers should be handled using legally recognized safeguards, such as contractual protections, platform controls, or equivalent mechanisms provided by the relevant service provider.

13. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures appropriate for the App's design, including minimizing the personal information collected by the App and storing learning data locally where practical. However, no app, device, network, or storage method is completely secure.

14. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, delete, or restrict personal information;
  • Object to or opt out of certain processing, including personalized advertising where applicable;
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent;
  • Request data portability where technically applicable;
  • Complain to a local data protection authority.

Because most App data is stored locally on your device, many requests can be handled by clearing App data, deleting saved drawings, resetting progress, or uninstalling the App. You may also contact us using the details below.
